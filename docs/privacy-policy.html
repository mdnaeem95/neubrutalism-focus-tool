Privacy Policy

Overview

Fokus ("the App") is a Pomodoro timer and task management application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

TL;DR We don't collect your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

Fokus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising

Local Data Storage

All app data — including your tasks, timer settings, statistics, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use a future cloud backup feature as a Pro subscriber.

Subscriptions

Fokus offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions ("Fokus Pro") managed entirely through Apple's App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status.

  • Payment processing is handled by Apple. We never see or store your payment details.
  • RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID to track subscription entitlements. No personal data is shared.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your device's Settings > Subscriptions.

Notifications

Fokus may request permission to send local notifications to alert you when timer sessions end. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not sent through any external server.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is:

  • RevenueCat — for subscription management. Their privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

Fokus does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since the app collects no personal data from any user, it is compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
